Bibliometric Analysis of Ice-Core Science: Mapping the Evolution of a Discipline

arXiv · 2409.15094 · 2024

A comprehensive bibliometric study examining the intellectual structure and geographic distribution of ice-core research. Applies guided machine learning for dynamic topic classification to trace how this interdisciplinary field has evolved across decades of scientific literature.
